What the App does

Tallyr lets customers collect loyalty stamps from participating cafes and redeem rewards. Baristas use the App to scan a customer QR code and record a stamp. Cafe owners use the App to configure their brand, cafes, loyalty cards, staff, and analytics.

Eligibility and accounts

You must be at least 13 years old to use the App. You are responsible for the activity on your account and for keeping access to your email inbox (used for sign-in codes). If you lose access to your email, contact info@tallyr.com.au.

Loyalty stamps and rewards

Stamps are issued by cafes, not by Tallyr. Each cafe or brand sets its own stamp goal and reward. A stamp is valid only when added by an authorised barista using the in-app scanner. Stamps and rewards have no cash value, are not transferable, and are only redeemable in accordance with the issuing cafe’s rules.

Cafes may change reward terms, pause a loyalty card, or close their account. When they do, existing stamps on that card may no longer be redeemable. Tallyr is not responsible for the underlying promotion.

Owner subscriptions (Tallyr Pro)

This section applies if you sign up as a brand owner and start a Tallyr Pro plan. Customers who collect stamps remain free — only owners pay for business tools.

Free trial — new brands receive a 30-day free trial with $0 due today. Full owner access (cafes, loyalty cards, baristas, analytics, QR join) is included during the trial.

Price after trial — Tallyr Pro is AUD $25 per month per brand (excluding applicable taxes), billed monthly. The price shown in the App comes from our plan catalog and may change with reasonable notice before it applies to you.

Payment processing (v1) — online card payments are not yet enabled in the App. When your trial ends, we will move you into our billing lifecycle and contact you by email. Once payment collection is enabled, you will add a payment method in Plan & billing.

Auto-renewal (when payment is enabled) — after you add a valid payment method, your subscription renews automatically each billing period at the then-current price unless you cancel before the renewal date.

Grace period — if payment cannot be processed, your account enters a 15-day grace period. During grace, stamping, redemptions, and owner tools continue to work. We send email reminders before grace ends.

Suspension when expired — if payment is not resolved after the grace period, your plan expires. New stamps, redemptions, and certain owner setup actions are paused until billing is restored. Customers who already hold your loyalty cards can still view their cards; we do not delete their stamp history.

Cancellation — until self-serve cancellation is available in the App, email info@tallyr.com.au from your owner account email to cancel or pause before your trial ends or before a renewal once payment is enabled.
